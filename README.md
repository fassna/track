### Project Description
EcoTrack is a smart waste reporting and reuse awareness platform that promotes sustainable waste management through community participation and reward-based engagement.

### The Problem statement
Improper waste disposal, lack of recycling awareness, and absence of an easy reporting mechanism contribute to environmental pollution and health hazards.  

Many citizens are unaware of:
- Where to report unmanaged waste  
- How to recycle different types of waste  
- How to reuse everyday materials instead of discarding them  

This leads to increased landfill waste, environmental degradation, and loss of reusable resources.
